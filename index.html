About Me

Hello! I am Zihad Hasan, a passionate Assistant Trainer specializing in Generative AI, driven by a profound commitment to education and skill development. My enthusiasm extends beyond the classroom; I am deeply engaged with the potential of Generative AI and emerging technologies, frequently sharing my insights and knowledge, often through writing. Currently, I contribute to this passion at the As-Sunnah Skill Development Institute, an affiliated institution of the As-Sunnah Foundation, where I lead the course "Generative AI with Digital Literacy," aiming to empower learners with crucial future-ready skills.

In addition to my teaching responsibilities, I am actively involved in multifaceted media, documentation, and coordination work for SBMC (Skill-Based Mentorship Center, or specify if different). My contributions here are diverse, encompassing the full spectrum of content creation from script development, video production, and photoshoots to meticulous editing. I also play a key role in organizing impactful events and preparing compelling online content for both academic and promotional purposes, thereby supporting various academic activities and significantly contributing to the institute’s public presence. This dynamic role includes the diligent compilation and presentation of daily task reports, covering SBMC's media activities—such as videography, photography, and content creation—as well as providing coordination support and reporting for the Skill Development Institute.

My technical acumen is further complemented by a strong foundation in the MS Office Suite (with particular strength in Excel), web fundamentals like HTML and CSS, general programming concepts, and effective technical troubleshooting. Demonstrating a proactive approach to continuous learning and practical application, I am currently developing a professional snake game in Python, complete with engaging features like level and mode changes. This personal project not only hones my programming capabilities but also reflects my dedication to applying technical skills in creative and challenging ways, always seeking to expand my expertise and contribute meaningfully.
